My Homelab

My homelab is one of my main hobbies. Even with having very limited funds for what I really want I still feel its fairly solid.

What I run:

Brokkr2:
My main server Brokkr2 or New Brokkr runs all my essential services such as my nas, syncthing, and jellyfin.

Brokkr2 Specs:
OS: Proxmox VE
CPU: Core i5 3470
RAM: 20GB DDR3 (3x4gb + 1x8gb)
STORAGE: x4 1TB WD BLUE HDD RAID5

Kvasir:
This is my secondary server runnings things to make jellyfin much cooler such as qbittorrent, sonarr, radarr, bazarr, jackett, and tdarr

Kvasir Specs:
OS: Proxmox VE
CPU: Pentium J2900
RAM: 16GB SODIMM DDR3
STORAGE: 1TB WD BLUE HDD
